FAQ for Advertisers

1. How can I create a campaign?

You can create a campaign by navigating to the "Create Campaign" page, filling out the required details (name, content type, platform, etc.), and submitting it for approval.

2. What types of campaigns can I create?

You can create campaigns for apps (Android, iOS, or both), websites, WhatsApp, Telegram, or other supported platforms. You can choose between video and image content types.

3. How are campaign costs calculated?

Campaign costs are calculated based on the number of views you select and the view preference (high or low). Higher view preferences may cost more per thousand views.

4. How do I track the performance of my campaigns?

You can track your campaign performance by navigating to the "My Campaigns" section on Create Campaign Page or visit "My Account" there you find My Campaign section. Here, you'll find details about views, engagement, and other metrics.

5. Can I edit or cancel a campaign after submission?

Once a campaign is submitted, you may not be able to edit it directly. However, you can contact support for assistance with modifications or cancellations.

6. How long does it take for my campaign to go live?

Once submitted, all campaigns are typically reviewed within 24 hours. You will be notified via email once your campaign is live.

7. How do I create a banner?

You can create a banner by navigating to the "Create Banner" page. Upload your banner image (with required dimensions), specify the target link, and select the duration for your banner ad.

8. What dimensions should my banner have?

The recommended dimensions for banners are 250x100 pixels. Ensure your image is clear and adheres to our content guidelines.

9. How do I book a weekly contest slot?

Visit the "Weekly Contest" page to view available slots. Select a week, provide the contest theme, description, and requirements, and submit your booking.

Our auction system allows user to bid for a items hosted by you. The highest bidder wins the items. To host an Auction Contact support for more details.

You can add funds to your account by visiting the "My Account" there tou will find "Add Cash" section. Choose your preferred payment method and follow the instructions.
